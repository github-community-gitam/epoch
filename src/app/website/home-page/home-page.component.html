Epoch 1.0 is the most happening tech event, organised by GitHub community. It's a two day fest conducted by GCG at GITAM University,Bangalore. The fest's primary focus will be on technology among students in GITAM. Epoch 1.0 introduces lot of fun activities and games not just related to tech, but also non-technical.

There are four main events :

  • Technical Treasure Hunt
  • LAN Gaming
  • Product Commercial Videography
  • Tech Trivia
These four events are conducted on all three campuses before the fest. The finalists in each event compete with others in the Bangalore campus. We also have a number of food stalls with varieties of tasty food where all the foodies can have fun trying out the food.
